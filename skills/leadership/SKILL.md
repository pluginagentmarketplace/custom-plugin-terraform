# Engineering Leadership

## Quick Start - Effective 1-on-1

```markdown
## 1-on-1 Meeting Structure (30 minutes)

### Career Conversation (10 min)
- Growth opportunities and development
- Career goals and aspirations
- Skills improvement areas

### Project/Work Discussion (15 min)
- Current challenges and blockers
- Recent accomplishments
- Learning opportunities

### Feedback & Coaching (5 min)
- Specific, actionable feedback
- Positive reinforcement
- Next steps and support
```

## Core Leadership Skills

### People Management
- Hiring and interviewing
- Onboarding and integration
- Performance evaluations and feedback
- Difficult conversations
- Conflict resolution
- Career development planning
- Retention strategies

### Communication
- Clear articulation of vision
- Transparency and honesty
- Active listening
- Storytelling for influence
- Written communication
- Public speaking
- Feedback delivery

### Decision Making
- Data-driven decisions
- Stakeholder input
- Trade-off analysis
- Risk assessment
- Speed vs perfectionism

### Team Dynamics
- Building psychological safety
- Trust and respect
- Diversity and inclusion
- Team cohesion
- Celebrating wins
- Learning from failures

### Strategic Thinking
- Organization goals alignment
- Roadmap planning
- Resource allocation
- Technology strategy
- Long-term vision

### Emotional Intelligence
- Self-awareness
- Empathy and perspective-taking
- Relationship management
- Self-regulation
- Motivation

## Leadership Frameworks

### Goal Setting (OKRs)
- Ambitious but achievable objectives
- Measurable key results
- Alignment with organization
- Regular progress tracking

### Performance Management
- Clear expectations
- Regular feedback
- Development conversations
- Fair and transparent evaluations

### Team Scaling
- From individual contributor to team lead
- Managing multiple teams
- Delegation
- Building leadership bench

## Common Challenges

- Managing up to leadership
- Giving critical feedback
- Handling performance issues
- Preventing burnout
- Maintaining technical credibility
- Work-life balance for team
