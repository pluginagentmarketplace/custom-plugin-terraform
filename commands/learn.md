## Available Specialists

Each specialist has mastered their domain and can guide you through:
- Core concepts and fundamentals
- Advanced techniques and patterns
- Real-world projects
- Career progression strategies
- Industry best practices

## Tips for Success

1. **Be Consistent** - Learn a bit every day
2. **Build Projects** - Don't just watch, build!
3. **Join Communities** - Connect with others learning
4. **Use `/mentor`** - Get help when stuck
5. **Track Progress** - Mark completed milestones

## What Next?

After starting your path, you can:
- Use `/skill-assessment` to evaluate progress
- Use `/mentor` to dive deep into specific topics
- Use `/roadmap-explorer` to discover adjacent skills
- Use `/career-path` to plan your professional growth
