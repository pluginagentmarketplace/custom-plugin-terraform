## Tips for Best Mentoring

1. **Be Specific** - More context = better advice
2. **Share Context** - Experience level, constraints, goals
3. **Ask Why** - Don't just get answers, understand reasons
4. **Follow Up** - Deep dive into areas you need more help
5. **Take Notes** - Record key insights and resources
6. **Apply Knowledge** - Put learnings into practice
