# /assess

Assess your current knowledge and get personalized recommendations.

## Quick Assessment (2-3 minutes)

Answer questions about your experience:

1. **Years of experience**: 0-2, 2-5, 5+
2. **Primary language**: Python, JavaScript, Go, Java, other
3. **Experience with**:
   - Databases (SQL, NoSQL)
   - APIs (REST, GraphQL)
   - System design
   - DevOps/Infrastructure
   - Security
   - Testing

4. **Current role**:
   - Student/Learner
   - Junior Developer
   - Mid-level Developer
   - Senior Developer
   - DevOps Engineer
   - Other

## Your Results

Based on assessment, you'll receive:

✅ **Skill Summary**
- Strengths
- Areas for growth
- Next 3 skills to master

✅ **Personalized Roadmap**
- Recommended learning path
- Estimated time commitment
- Projects to build

✅ **Agent Recommendation**
- Best specialist for you
- Specific topics to focus on
- Learning resources

## Reassess Regularly

- Monthly: Track progress
- Quarterly: Update goals
- Annually: Long-term planning

Use `/learn` to follow your personalized path!
