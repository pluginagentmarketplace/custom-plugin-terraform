# /assess - Skill Assessment & Level Evaluation

**Evaluate your current skill level across all 7 specializations and get personalized improvement recommendations.**

Get honest assessments, identify knowledge gaps, and receive targeted guidance to advance your skills.

## How Assessment Works

When you run `/assess`, you'll:

1. **Take Quick Skill Evaluations** (5-10 minutes each):
   - 10-15 targeted questions per specialization
   - Multiple difficulty levels
   - Practical scenario-based questions
   - Real-world problem analysis

2. **Receive Detailed Results**:
   - Current skill level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced)
   - Proficiency score (0-100%)
   - Specific strength areas
   - Knowledge gaps and weaknesses
   - Time estimate to next level

3. **Get Improvement Roadmap**:
   - Personalized learning path
   - Specific topics to focus on
   - Recommended projects
   - Resource recommendations
   - Timeline for improvement

4. **Track Progress Over Time**:
   - Compare assessments month-to-month
   - Celebrate improvements
   - Identify acceleration areas

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When should I retake the assessment?
- **Monthly** during intensive learning (first 6 months)
- **Quarterly** after foundation is solid
- **Before major decisions** (career changes, specialization)

### How is my score calculated?
- Combination of correct answers and explanation quality
- Partial credit for partially correct answers
- Bonus points for optimized solutions
- No time pressure on answers

### What if I disagree with my assessment?
- Explain your reasoning in detail
- Provide additional context or examples
- Reassess specific areas where you disagree
- Use actual project results to validate

### How do I improve my score fastest?
1. Work on weakest specialization first
2. Complete projects in that area
3. Code review and learn from others
4. Read books/articles on specific topics
5. Monthly reassessment to validate improvement
